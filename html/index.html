About

All models below have been trained as follows:

1.Dataset is split as 90% training and 10% testing

2.A GoogleNet is initialized with ILSVRC12 weights (aka 1000 classes from Imagenet)

3.Network is trained in between 50000 and 300000 steps with step control of the learning rate and SGD or Nesterov as optimizer

4.Data augmentation varies from mirroring to mirroring + rotations

If you have difficulties finetuning the models, contact us.
